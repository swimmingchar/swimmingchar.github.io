About Mother's Day

Learn more about this special day dedicated to honoring mothers everywhere

📅

The Origin

Mother's Day traces back to ancient Greek festivals honoring maternal goddesses. The modern holiday was pioneered by Anna Jarvis, becoming an official U.S. holiday in 1914.

🌷

The Carnation

The carnation is the official flower of Mother's Day. Red carnations represent a living mother's love, while white ones honor mothers who have passed away.

💝

Ways to Celebrate

A bouquet, a warm hug, a home-cooked meal, or simply saying "I love you, Mom" — love doesn't need to be expensive, just heartfelt.
